RULES OF PONG GAME!

Welcome to Pong Transcendence!

Game Modes:

  • Solo: Play classic Pong, but each point is represented by a life.
  • Rumble: A four-player experience where each player is eliminated when they lose all their lives. The game continues until only one player remains.
  • Tournaments: The Rumble mode is played with a defined number of games. Players earn points after each game:
    • 1st place: 10 points
    • 2nd place: 5 points
    • 3rd place: 3 points
    The winner is the player with the highest cumulative score. In case of a tie, an additional game is played.

For a better experience in Rumble, use two keyboards.

Gameplay Options:

  • Number of lives for players
  • View:
    • Top: classic top-down view
    • Side: side view with enhanced 3D effects
  • Paddle width
  • Power-up: after three bounces, the green light indicates that the power-up is ready. When activated, the paddle teleports in front of the ball and follows it.

Player controls:

  • Player 1:
    • Movement: W, S
    • Power-up: Q
  • Player 2:
    • Movement: Arrow UP, Arrow DOWN
    • Power-up: Arrow RIGHT
  • Player 3:
    • Movement: H, J
    • Power-up: G
  • Player 4:
    • Movement: 7, 8
    • Power-up: 9
